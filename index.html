About Oddes AB

Oddes is a certified solar and energy contractor working with over 40 municipalities across Sweden. We are one of the largest providers of BESS (Battery Energy Storage Systems) to the public sector and cooperate with leading suppliers such as Huawei and others through centralized EU procurement.

Swedish Energy Market Outlook

Sweden targets net-zero emissions by 2045. The electrification of transportation, industry, and society as a whole will significantly increase electricity demand in the coming decade. Energy storage will play a key role in grid stability and arbitrage opportunities.
